About Us

Quality Scans is an initiative by team of quality-oriented doctors. The diagnosis center commenced its services to the public in the year 2022 founded by Rtn.Mr. Rajkumar.P with the sole objective of providing reliable and quality service at an affordable cost.

The diagnosis center is equipped with high-end imported machines capable of providing very accurate and faster report. Our diagnosis center is designed in a way to eliminate human errors and provide accurate reports. The diagnosis center aims to provide accessible, affordable, timely and quality healthcare diagnostics, applying insights and cutting-edge technology to create value for all kinds of people.
